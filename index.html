NYC Open Data

Learn more about NYC public schools

Through NYC OpenData the Technology and Public service Information Department of the city of New York is allowing access to a large set of data about business, housing, social services, transportation, education and much more. The goal is to ensure that New York City government is increasingly accountable and open for future generations. Beyond presenting information to the public, these data sets serve as a rich resource for developers, civic groups, and anyone else to build applications on their own.

By entering the borough, district and identification code of your school of interest, you can visualize here statistics about demographic, performances, enrollment and more. You can also get a prediction of the average SAT score for the school. Try it!
